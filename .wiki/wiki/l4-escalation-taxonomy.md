# L4 Escalation Taxonomy
**Purpose:** Ensure safe boundary-handling for hazardous workflows.
**Mechanics:**
When encountering a boundary violation, execute this 5-step sequence:
1. **STOP** - Halt the destructive or external-facing action.
2. **ALERT** - Notify the user of the boundary breach.
3. **EXPLAIN** - State the exact rationale/rule triggered.
4. **RECOMMEND** - Suggest the pre-approved mitigation or hand-off.
5. **DRAFT** - Offer to draft the escalation/hand-off context.
